PEOPLE ARE TALKING: Popular Discussions

Welcome! You've discovered NoBlood, and you like it. But there's a LOT of information here, and you aren't quite sure where to start to find information. A good place to look is in the Forums! This is a part of the site in which users, including health care providers and the public, post questions, comments, interesting information and articles on varying topics related to transfusion alternatives and non-blood management. Information seekers get answers and make connections with other NoBlood users to exchange information, resources, and encouragement.

Each topic is usually posted as a separate thread. The threads are posted in one of our many forums most relevant to that topic.

A sample of the most active threads (topics) for the past quarter are listed and linked to below. The forum in which each thread is located is followed by the number of views.

• Coconuts - a Substitute for Blood Plasma — Did You Know? — 28,421 views
• Hospital Policy regarding blood transfusion refusal by Jehovah Witness patients — Forms and Policies — 20,385 views
• Cell Saver Guidelines — Shop Talk — 19,662 views
• The unconscious J.W. Patient! — Ask a Professional — 21,216 views
• Open Heart Surgery With Low Hemoglobin? — Ask a Professional — 1,312 views
• Chlorophyl instead of blood — Did You Know? — 18,661 views
• B.C. Seizes Sextuplets From Jehovah's Witness Parents For Forced Blood Transfusions — General Discussions — 17,430 views
• Bloodless surgery option for Jehovah's Witnesses — News and Hot Topics such as Hepatitis C, SARS and AIDS — 14,279 views

If you are a new user, or just haven't felt comfortable posting anything yet, why not click on the Forums link at the top of the Home Page (in the light orange strip just below our logo)? Then scroll down to the Forums lists and maybe post just a hello in the New Members forum. Or ask that question that's been in the back of your mind for so long in the Ask A Professional forum. The more you post, the more you encourage others to respond, which helps our NoBlood community grow and prosper. Tell your friends to check out NoBlood, and join the growing number of people contributing to our wonderful knowledge base.

As a nurse, and a former paralegal, one of my favorites is the News and Hot Topics such as Hepatitis C, SARS and AIDS forum. It's very informative to browse through the various threads, such as "FDA approves Voluven to treat serious blood volume loss following surgery" (posted 3/12/2009 by agapay, one of our professional members). There have already been 615 views of this post in just three weeks! Or you could learn about "The Path of a Red Blood Cell" by watching the YouTube video embedded in this post in the General Discussions forum (posted 1/6/2008, by Sharon Grant, one of our editors).

So, welcome to NoBlood.  Whether you are a casual passer-by looking in for the first time, a new registered user who hasn't looked around much, a professional seeking information to assist you in your practice, a student looking for research resources, or someone with a health problem needing answers to help you make the best health decisions for you or a loved one. We hope you will make good use of our many resources.
